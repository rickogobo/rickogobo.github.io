Car Pool

Breakfast starts at 7:00 am at the dining complex on campus. ILRN will start every day ay 8:30. You have plenty of options to get on campus, including walking, Uber/Lyft or asking someone to drive you. The local transport doesn't start before 8 am, unfortunetally. However, the local chairs of the conference will drive several times between the hotels on Monterey Street and campus. If you need a ride, put your request some 15 min prior on the discord channel. Note, we do our best but cannot guarantee having a spot for everyone in time.
You must have Discord installed and have access to the iLRN server!
