The iLRN2023 takes place on the campus of the California Polytechnic State University San Luis Obispo, in short SLO.
On campus, note three important locations: Main lecture room 03-213, secondary lecture room 03-113 and dining commons (breakfast and lunch). Both lecture rooms are located inside the Business Building, building #3.
